How to Play

Move your bucket left and right to catch blue water droplets.
Avoid green and black droplets.

Controls:
Arrow keys (desktop) or swipe (mobile) to move.
Pause with the pause button.

Scoring:
Blue = +1 point, Green = -1 point, Black = game over.

Blue Streaks: Every 5 blue drops in a row doubles your points per blue drop (2x, 4x, 8x, etc.).
Green Penalties: Each consecutive green drop increases the penalty (-1, -2, -3, -4, etc.).
Hitting green ends blue streaks. Green penalties persist until game over.

Try to beat your high score!
